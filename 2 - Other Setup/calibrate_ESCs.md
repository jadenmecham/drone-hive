# Video Instructions
Reference [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l5tjRe4CkL4).
Make sure to plug the motor into port 1 of the X8R. Also only need power and ground wires on the sbus port.

# Written Instrustions
1. Makes sure that the X8R receiver is already paired to the controller
2. Turn off the controller
3. Plug motor into ESC
4. Plug the ESC into port 1 of the X8R receiver
5. Turn on the controller and make sure the thrust stick is all the way down
6. Power the receiver. Once connected to the controller, it will have a green flashing light
7. Move the thrust stick from all the way down to all the way up and back down
